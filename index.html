Interest in IT

The concept of artificial intelligence (A.I.) had always fascinated me. This interest spurred from various depictions of robots and A.I.s in novels, movies, and games. While I am curious and intrigued with the development of an A.I., specifically one with sentience, the furthest I have gotten in terms of IT experience is a high school elementary web design course, where I studied fundamental basics of HTML.
I chose to study at RMIT for its new and unique T&I and Language program, which is allegedly far more practical than programs offered by other Universities in Vietnam. Furthermore, the University also supports students with their internships with its diverse connection, including Japanese industry leaders in the IT field. And rare are such opportunities that I cannot forego the merits RMIT provides.
It is my goal to not only learn and master the linguistic skills necessary for my T&I profession, but also to familiarize with technical skills that may be of assistance. The world is moving fast. Thus, we must adapt as to not be outpaced.
